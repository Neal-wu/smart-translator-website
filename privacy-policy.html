Privacy Policy for Smart Translator

Effective Date: August 21, 2025

Introduction

Smart Translator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Smart Translator mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • We do not collect any personal information from users
  • No user accounts or profiles are created
  • No personal data is stored on our servers

Usage Information

  • Translation text is processed locally on your device
  • No translation data is transmitted to our servers
  • We do not track your translation history or usage patterns

Device Information

  • Basic device information for app functionality
  • No unique device identifiers are collected
  • No location data is accessed or stored

How We Use Information

Translation Services

  • Text translations are processed locally on your device
  • No translation data is sent to external servers
  • All translation processing happens in real-time on your device

App Functionality

  • App features work entirely offline
  • No data is required for basic app operation
  • No analytics or tracking is implemented

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Translation data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is uploaded to our servers
  • You have complete control over your data

Security Measures

  • No network transmission of user data
  • No external API calls for translation services
  • App operates in a secure, offline environment

Third-Party Services

No Third-Party Services

  • We do not use third-party analytics
  • No advertising networks are integrated
  • No social media plugins are included
  • No external translation APIs are used

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Data Control

  • You have complete control over your data
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • You can delete the app to remove all local data

No Data Collection

  • We do not collect, store, or process personal data
  • No opt-out procedures are needed
  • No data deletion requests are necessary

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

App Store Compliance

This app complies with Apple's App Store guidelines regarding privacy and data collection. The app:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track user behavior
  • Does not share data with third parties
  • Operates entirely offline for user privacy

California Privacy Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) does not apply to our data practices.

European Privacy Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) does not apply to our data practices.
